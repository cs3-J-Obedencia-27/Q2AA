Academic Programs

One thing about PSHS-CRC is that it lies in its comprehensive academic programs. The curriculum challenges the students' intellect, developing more appreciation on sciences. The curriculum is designed and is ensured to keep an insightful education for the students.

Extracurricular Activities

Acknowledging the significance of integrated or holistic education, extracurricular activities are very important. Students are encouraged to participate in activities where their true potential can be exhibited and explore their interests in various fields such as physics, robotics, or computer programming. Engaging in such activities enhances not only academic skills but also critical thinking, creativity, leadership, and teamwork.

Research and Innovations

Another one of the distinguishing features of PSHS-CRC is the commitment to research and innovation. The students actively take part in research projects, working with faculty members or research experts.

Community Outreach

PSHS-CRC is proud of its community service. Students participate in outreach activities where they learn to use their voice to speak about societal concerns, while also using their scientific knowledge to analyze real-world problems.

Success Stories

PSHS-CRC has produced numerous successful stories. Graduates have excelled in their fields internationally and globally, having a bright future ahead of them. Many PSHS-CRC alumni have become trailblazers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), contributing significantly to the advancement of knowledge and innovation.
